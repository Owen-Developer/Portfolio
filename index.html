Frequently Asked Questions
+
What services do I offer?
I create high-quality portfolios and agency websites, specializing in design and development. Additional services such as SEO, updates, or maintenance are available on request.
+
How much does a website cost?
I create your website at no upfront cost. You only pay $15 (free hosting) if you’re completely satisfied with the final design and functionality. If you're happy, it's yours!
+
How do you build your websites?
I design using Figma and code the websites with HTML, CSS, JS, and React.js. These technologies allow me to build responsive, high-quality websites tailored to your needs.
+
How long does a project take?
Projects, understandably, vary in length.

An average project (single landing page) can take 2 or 3 days accounting for ideation, revisions etc.

2. Mood board

3. Wireframe

4. Design

5. Development
